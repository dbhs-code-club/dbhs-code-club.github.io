Purpose

Code Club (C2) is an organization at Diamond Bar High School where members can expand their coding skills and interests.

See the PDF of the club constitution.

Activities

We are planning to hold workshops weekly in the coming school year. Topics include Capture the Flag (CTFs), computer science, and web development.

Our first meeting will be after Club Rush, which is the third week of school.

We are still in the process of planning and setting up everything. More details will be released soon! In the meantime, why not sign up?
