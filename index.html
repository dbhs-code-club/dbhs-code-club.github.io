What is Code Club?

Code Club (C2) is an organization at Diamond Bar High School where members can expand their coding skills and interests.

Who is welcome?

Everyone who has an interest in coding or computer science may join. Although not everyone ought to be a programmer, we encourage people to try and see if you like this stuff.

What will we do?

We are planning to hold workshops weekly in the coming school year. Topics include Capture the Flag (CTFs), computer science, and web development. We will also participate together (or individually) in CTFs and hackathons.
