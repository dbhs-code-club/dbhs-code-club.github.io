Code Club (C2)

Purpose

Code Club is an organization at Diamond Bar High School where members can expand their coding skills and interests.

See the PDF of the club constitution.

Activities

We are planning to hold workshops weekly in the coming school year. Topics include Capture the Flag (CTFs), computer science, and web development.

Can't wait to join? Our first meeting will be after Club Rush, which is the third week of school.

We are still in the process of planning and setting up everything. More details and the sign up form will be released soon!
